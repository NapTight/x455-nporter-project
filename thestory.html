HOW WE CAME TO BE

In the heart of a vibrant beach town, a group of passionate artists came together to form "Form and Photo." Led by Nick, a humble visionary, they embarked on a creative journey that would meld the world of cannabis and furniture design.

Nick, driven by a genuine love for his craft, poured his heart and soul into crafting remarkable furniture pieces. His designs, though unassuming, radiated a unique blend of beauty and functionality that resonated with people.

Joined by skilled artisans Sarah, Lisa, and Alex, the team of Form and Photo created a harmonious collaboration. Sarah's craftsmanship brought Nick's designs to life with impeccable attention to detail. Lisa's insights into market trends added an element of practicality to their creations. Alex's photography captured the essence of their work, unveiling its hidden beauty to the world.

Form and Photo humbly set out to make a difference in the design industry. Their passion and dedication spoke volumes, and their creations found their way into homes, galleries, and spaces that appreciated their artistry.

Today, Form and Photo continue to embrace their humble roots. They strive to create designs that touch people's lives, celebrating the marriage of form and functionality. Join them on their artistic journey as they transform spaces and create meaningful experiences through their heartfelt designs.
